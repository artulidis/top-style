our mission

In Top Style Salon we celebrate every individual for their real, most raw, authentic brilliance, who believe in themselves and are ready to step into the look they love. We focus on quality and comfort by employing the best hair products with some of the most natural and rich ingredients because we believe you should never expect less. Whether you're looking for a simple trim, exotic color change, make-up learning lessons, or a style, Kate's team's demeanor will instantly put you at ease, helping to focus the attention on YOUR top requests, YOUR top look, & YOUR top style. 100% guaranteed.

our stylists

meet our stylists

At Top Style Salon, all of our stylists are licensed professionals, continuously educating themselves with the latest hair trends. We love going to annual fashion conventions and taking numerous online and in-person classes. We are proud of being well known for doing detailed consultations with each and every guest to ensure we consistently achieve the most possible desired look. Every team member follows strict sanitation requirements and high standards of cleaning and disinfection to provide a healthy environment for our guests. Our salon also utilizes the latest technology, including personally written text messages, emails, social media direct messages, and other online communication to make sure we are easily reachable 7 days a week. We strive for the highest customer service and for top quality work. Thank you for supporting our small but awesome team! We look forward to seeing you!

our policies

Top Style's Satisfaction Policy

At Top Style, your happiness is our Top priority. We want you to love your new look! If you're not completely satisfied with our services please let us know directly within 2 weeks of service date for any adjustments. We do not offer refunds, however we will do our absolute best to achieve your desired style...

Top Style's Missed Appointment and Cancellation Policy

If you are unable to keep a scheduled appointment please give 24 hours advance notice, To ensure that you will not be charged for the appointment. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice are subjects to a fee of 50% of scheduled service.

*You will be asked for credit card on file prior scheduling your service*
Covid-19 Policy

At Top Style we are continually focused on the health and well-being of our team and our guests to ensure a safe and healthy environment for everyone. We have followed the direction of the CDC by implementing strict rules:wearing masks, temperature checks, UV-lights air filters, high standards of sanitation. Prior your appointment with 2-weeks and 2-days conformation texts you will receive safety guidelines information. Thank you for working Together to keep everyone SAFE!
